Listings, Certification & Renewals

The RAD Ledger separates Certified projects from Verified projects.

  • Certified listings are permanent unless downgraded for cause (rug, fraud, or violation).
  • Verified listings are annual and must renew every 12 months to stay in good standing.
  • Each renewal goes through the same vetting process as the initial listing to confirm nothing has changed.

When a Verified project does not renew on time, its status is treated as Expired until it passes review again.
