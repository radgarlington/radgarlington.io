Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 2025

RAD GARLINGTON ($RAD) operates radgarlington.io and an OTC “Buy” flow on the XRP Ledger (XRPL). This page explains what we collect and how we use it.

What we don’t collect

  • No accounts or passwords
  • No private keys or seed phrases
  • No custody of your funds

What we may collect

  • Basic server logs (IP, user-agent, path) for uptime & abuse prevention
  • Quote/payment parameters (USD amount, timestamp) to generate links and prevent fraud
  • On-chain data (public by design): payment amounts, addresses, tx hashes
  • Xaman (XUMM) webhooks if enabled (signed/expired status, tx hash) for reconciliation

How we use info

  • Operate the site and API
  • Generate quotes & payment requests
  • Reconcile payments and deliver RAD
  • Detect & prevent abuse

Cookies & analytics

We do not use invasive tracking. If we add privacy-respecting analytics later, we’ll update this page.
